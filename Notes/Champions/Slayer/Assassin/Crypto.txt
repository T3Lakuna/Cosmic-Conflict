Name
	Crypto, the Rogue Hacker
Class/Role
	Assassin
Origin, Theme, Concept, Backstory, and References.
	Dystopian cyberpunk prodigy. League of Legends PROJECT skin line.
Passive Ability/Abilities
	Attacks deal extra damage to targets based on their missing health.
Normal Ability/Abilities
	- Crypto's next basic attack slows the target and reduces their resistances.
	- Area-of-effect attack which slows enemies and silences enemies near the center.
	- Crypto becomes invisible, summoning a copy of herself which walks towards the cursor. Can be cast again to swap places with the clone.
Ultimate Ability/Abilities
	Area-of-effect attack based on Crypto's location which deals a large amount of damage and silences enemies.
Strengths
	High burst damage, very high mobility, good stealth, defensive tricks, good at fighting targets who are alone, great matchup into mages and marksmen.
Weaknesses, Counters, and Counterplay
	Low crowd-control, no disengage, low durability, bad matchup into high-durability opponents and enemies with shields, bad matchup into tanks and fighters.
