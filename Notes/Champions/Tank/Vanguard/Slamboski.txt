Name
	Slambowski, The Valley Shaper
Role
	Vanguard
Origin, Theme, Concept, Backstory, and References.
	Slambowski was the landshaper of the war planet Ignia. He shaped the lands and went into slumber once life began. He awoke to a wasteland instead of his paradise.
Passive Ability
	After casting any ability, Slambowski creates a shockwave around him that deals low damage and stuns enemies for a very short amount of time.
Normal Abilities
	- Slambowski slams the ground and creates a earthen wall after 1 second. Interacts like Trundle pillar.
	- Slambowski slams his fists together to lower the resistances of enemies in front of him.
	- Slambowski climbs up a wall. Can be recast to launch him off the wall, dealing damage to enemies in the way.
Ultimate Ability
	Slambowski winds up a punch. The longer the punch is wound up, the more damage it does. Any crowd-control will cause Slamboski to activate his ulti (he cannot manually cancel it).
Strengths
	Very high durability, very high crowd-control, great engage, good disruption/diversion, good lockdown, good tools to increase defenses, great matchup into assassins and mages.
Weaknesses, Counters, and Counterplay
	Melee, very low damage, low mobility, terrible in single combat, weak matchup into sustained damage from fighters and marksmen, no disengage.
